An Invitation to christ

Brothers and Sisters, in the midst of doubt and searching, this book is my testimony from hollow years of restlessness to the awakening light of Christ. it offers honest reflections on humility, purity, love, and intimate friendship with Jesus, then defends the truth of Scripture, the Trinity, and answers sincere questions including those from an Islamic perspective with clarity and respect.

May these words draw your heart closer to the Living Christ who satisfies every longing. Read, share freely, and encounter Him.
